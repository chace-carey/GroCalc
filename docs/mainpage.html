Welcome to GroCalc!

Info

What is GroCalc?

GroCalc is an app that helps you split costs of groceries and more amongst your roommates.

First, create an account with an email and a password. Then verify that account in your emails and sign in to the app. Once you're in you can add your roommates as friends, create items with their name, cost, and which roommates are paying, and then you can calculate the total amount each roommate needs to pay.

Features

Sign Up

Firstly, you will want to sign up.

This can be done by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on the main page.

After you are finished entering in your information and pressing the "Sign Up" button, then you should recieve a confirmation email asking you to click the link to verify your email.

Sign In

Once you have verified your email, you can then re-enter your information to log in to your newly made account

Make Name

After you have clicked the "Sign In" button, you will be asked to enter your display name which will be used by your roommates to identify you.

Items Page

Now that you have signed in and created your account name, you will be directed to the items page.

This page is where you will be able to see all of your items once they have been added

Add Item

To start adding items, click the "Add Item" button on the items page.

You will then be directed to the "Add Item" page. Here, you can enter the name of the item, the cost of that item, and the quantity of the item. You can then swipe right on the roommates which will be sharing this item with you.

Finally, click the "Add" button on the bottom of your screen in order to save that item, which can be confirmed by seeing the item appear on the items page after the button is pressed.

Item Details

The Item Details page is where you will be able to access and edit the information of the selected item.

To access this page, click on the item you wish to view and/or edit on the items page. Once here, you can edit the price and quantity of the item by clicking the "+" button to the right of the desired field, enter in the desired change, and then press the check mark to confirm this change.

You can also delete the selected item by clicking the "Delete Item" button at the bottom of the page.

Friends/Roommates

The friends page is where you will be able to add friends by entering their email and/or accept friend requests.

This page can be accessed by clicking the friend icon on the top right of the items page.

Then, if you wan to add a friend, ask them for their email address associated with their GroCalc account in order to enter the recieved email into the text entry box. Once entered, you can click the "Send" button, and your friend should recieve a friend request.
